# CALAMITYVILLE — BUSINESS
**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

## Monetization Strategy

### Premium Content ($4.99–$9.99/month)
Deep-dive narratives, historical photos, newspaper clippings, audio content behind each token. Free version gets basic descriptions. Premium gets the full story.

### Sponsored Locations
Businesses pay to become "Safe Zones" or token hotspots driving foot traffic. Framed as community hubs, not crime-adjacent.

### Media Licensing
Content database licensed to streaming services, podcast networks, true crime/history media. Co-produced content partnerships (YouTube series, podcast tie-ins, History Channel).

### Educational Licensing
Field trip version for schools and museums. Curated, age-appropriate historical content tied to curriculum standards.

### Advertising
Rewarded video ads, interstitials. Premium subscription removes ads.

### Merchandise
Physical token collectibles (enamel pins, trading cards). Partner with true crime/history media for branded content.
