# CALAMITYVILLE — CONCEPT
**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

## What It Is
A location-based historical event discovery platform and mobile game. Users explore real-world locations tied to significant historical events — crimes, disasters, scandals, tragedies, infamous broadcasts, industrial accidents, assassinations — collecting digital tokens, competing in weekly themed events, and climbing leaderboards.

## Concept Evolution
True Crime GO → MurderHouse GO! → Calamityville

## Key Pivot (March 22, 2026)
Expanded from crime-only to ALL historically significant events. Content restricted to historical events (decades old minimum). This solves player safety (no one sent to currently dangerous areas), victim sensitivity (no living victims), and content quality (older events have better stories).

## Core Mechanic
Historical events geocoded to real-world locations → collected as digital tokens → themed weekly competitive events → leaderboards

## Token Tiers
- **Common (Bronze):** Unlimited collection. Minor events.
- **Uncommon (Silver):** Limited per event cycle. Notable events.
- **Rare (Gold):** Very limited. Significant events. Pulse animation.
- **Ultra Rare (Platinum):** 1 per metro area per cycle. Major historical events.
- **Golden Unicorn:** 1 per state per cycle. Maximum prestige.

## Game Modes
- **Discovery:** Explore the map from anywhere, collect tokens remotely. Pull phone out in a new town, see a heat map of nearby historical events, collect as you go.
- **Explorer:** Physical visit bonus. Safe because all locations are historical (decades old). The Dalton Gang raid site in Coffeyville isn't dangerous — it's tourism.

## Weekly Events
Monday 9AM–Friday 9PM. Themed challenges: "Prohibition Week: 1920–1933," "Wild West Weekend," "Gilded Age Grift." Weekend events shorter and more intense.

## Age Tiers
- 13–17: Non-violent events only (property crimes, disasters, scandals)
- 18+: Full content (excludes sexual assault and crimes against children)

## Buffer Zones
All token positions offset 100–300ft from actual locations in random direction. Non-negotiable.

## Target Audience
True crime + history crossover. People who visit Tombstone AZ, take ghost tours, binge Dateline, read about Prohibition bootlegging. Curious people who like to explore and learn.
